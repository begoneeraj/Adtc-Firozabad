Step-by-Step

From Enrolment to Licence — A Clear Path

ADTC replaces the stress and uncertainty of the conventional RTO test process with a standardized, technology-driven evaluation that is transparent and fair.

  • Enrol at ADTC — submit documents and complete registration
  • Attend theory classes and simulator practice sessions
  • Complete practical driving on the automated test track
  • Pass automated evaluation — receive Form 5B certificate
  • Submit Form 5B to RTO — get your licence without a driving test

Why Choose ADTC

The Standard That Regular Driving Schools Cannot Match

ADTC is legally accredited, technologically advanced, and government-monitored — offering a path to your driving licence that is fair, skill-based, and corruption-free.

Government Accreditation

Legally recognized under CMVR Rules 31B–31J. Form 5B certificates are accepted by RTOs across all of India.

Automated, Objective Testing

RFID sensors and HD cameras score your performance. No human judgment, no bias, no possibility of bribery.

RTO Test Exemption

Form 5B holders are exempted from the RTO driving test under CMVR Rule 15 — a faster, direct route to your licence.

Official Inauguration

A Proud Milestone for Firozabad

It is a matter of immense pride to announce the successful inauguration of the ADTC Centre, Firozabad — a subsidiary of Kisaan Eicher Tractors, Shikohabad, and duly affiliated with the Government of Uttar Pradesh under the Central Motor Vehicles Rules 1989. The centre is now fully operational, dedicated to providing high-quality, standardized driver training, objective automated evaluation, and upholding the highest standards of excellence and road safety.
