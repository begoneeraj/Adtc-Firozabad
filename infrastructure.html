Facility & Infrastructure

ADTC Firozabad meets every standard prescribed under CMVR Rules 31B–31J — equipped with RFID sensors, HD cameras, driving simulators, and a fully automated test track for objective evaluation.

Physical Facility

Built to Government Standards

Every aspect of ADTC Firozabad's physical facility meets the minimum requirements prescribed in CMVR Rules 31B–31J. No shortcuts — because your evaluation depends on a properly configured environment.

Automated Test Track (ADTT)

Full-size driving test track with multiple evaluation scenarios, compliant with CMVR specifications.

Parallel Parking Bay

Standardized parking area with sensors to detect precision and completion without contact.

H-Track (Two-Wheelers)

Dedicated H-shaped track for two-wheeler evaluation — tests precision balance and turning.

S-Curve & Gradient

Curved section and gradient slope to evaluate control under diverse road conditions.

Air-Conditioned Classrooms

Modern classrooms with projectors, digital teaching aids, and seating for theory instruction.

Administrative Block

Reception, registration offices, record room, and candidate waiting area.

Technology

Automated Evaluation Technology

The automation that removes human bias from your evaluation — every sensor, camera, and system working together to give you a fair, objective score.

RFID Sensors

Embedded throughout the test track to monitor vehicle position, speed, lane adherence, and compliance in real time. Triggered automatically as the vehicle passes.

High-Definition CCTV Cameras

Multiple HD cameras covering every corner of the test track. Footage used for real-time scoring and is archived for dispute resolution and audits.

Advanced Driving Simulators

Realistic digital simulators replicating Indian road conditions — useful for pre-track practice, including night driving, rain, and emergency scenarios.

Real-Time Analytics Dashboard

Central system that aggregates RFID and camera data instantly. Scores are calculated automatically — no examiner manually marks the test.

National Database Connectivity

Directly linked to the Parivahan national database. Candidate records, Form 5B issuance, and evaluation results are submitted digitally in real time.

Candidate Verification System

Digital identity verification at the gate — ensures the correct candidate is taking the evaluation. Prevents proxy or impersonation.

Tamper-Proof Recording

All evaluation data is encrypted and stored securely. Records cannot be altered post-evaluation. Accessible by UP Transport Department for auditing.

Certified Instructors & Staff

All instructors hold CMVR-prescribed certifications. They undergo periodic re-evaluation. Staff records are maintained and available for inspection.
