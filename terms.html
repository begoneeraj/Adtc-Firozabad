10. Terms Specific to Enrolment and Training

10.1 Pre-Registration

Submission of the online pre-registration form does not constitute a confirmed enrolment. Final enrolment is completed in person at ADTC Firozabad with original documents. Slot allocation is subject to availability.

10.2 Eligibility

Candidates must meet the minimum age requirement under the Motor Vehicles Act 1988 and must possess the applicable Learner's Licence for LMV and commercial vehicle categories. ADTC Firozabad reserves the right to decline enrolment to candidates who do not meet legal eligibility requirements.

10.3 Conduct on Premises

All candidates and visitors must comply with the centre's safety rules, instructions of instructors and staff, and all applicable traffic laws while on the ADTC premises. Misconduct, providing false identity, or attempting to manipulate the automated evaluation system may result in immediate termination of enrolment without refund and may be reported to the UP Transport Department and law enforcement.

10.4 Fees

Course fees are as prescribed and communicated at the time of enrolment. Fees are non-refundable once training has commenced, except in cases of cancellation by ADTC Firozabad. Partial refunds for incomplete courses may be considered at the centre's discretion, subject to the portion of training completed.

10.5 Evaluation

The automated evaluation on the ADTT (Automated Driving Test Track) is scored objectively by sensors and cameras. Candidates accept that the automated score is final and binding for the purposes of Form 5B issuance. Disputes regarding evaluation scores must be raised with the Grievance Officer within 7 days of the evaluation date.

11. Form 5B — Conditions of Issuance

Form 5B is issued solely on the basis of successful completion of the ADTC training programme and passing the automated evaluation. ADTC Firozabad does not issue Form 5B:

  • Without completing the full prescribed training programme
  • On the basis of payment, influence, or any factor other than objective evaluation results
  • To candidates who have provided false identity or documentation
  • For vehicle categories for which the candidate has not completed category-specific training

Form 5B issued by ADTC Firozabad is a government-prescribed certificate under CMVR 1989 and carries the full legal weight of that authority. Misuse, forgery, or unauthorized reproduction of Form 5B is a criminal offence under the Indian Penal Code and the Motor Vehicles Act 1988.
