Enrolment Process

A clear, step-by-step guide to how training at ADTC Firozabad works — from your first visit through receiving your Form 5B certificate and ultimately your driving licence.

Step by Step

Your Journey from Candidate to Licence Holder

Six clear stages — each designed to build your skills progressively and ensure you are genuinely road-ready before certification.

01

Registration & Enrolment

Visit ADTC Firozabad. Submit required documents (identity proof, address proof, photographs, medical certificate). Complete registration and pay course fee. Receive enrolment confirmation and schedule.

02

Theory Classes

Attend structured classroom sessions covering traffic rules and road signs, vehicle mechanics and operation, road safety practices, right-of-way rules, and emergency procedures.

03

Simulator Training

Practice in our advanced driving simulator — experience diverse road conditions, night driving, rain, heavy traffic, and emergency scenarios in a completely safe environment.

04

Practical Track Training

Supervised driving practice on the ADTT — master parallel parking, H-track (two-wheelers), S-curve, gradient slope, reversing, and lane discipline under instructor guidance.

05

Automated Evaluation

Final assessment on the Automated Driving Test Track. RFID sensors, HD cameras, and real-time analytics score every manoeuvre objectively. Results are immediate and fully transparent.

06

Form 5B & RTO Licence

Pass evaluation — receive Form 5B certificate from ADTC. Submit it to your local RTO along with other documents. The RTO issues your driving licence. No RTO driving test required.

Pathways

What Happens After Evaluation

Two clear outcomes — both ultimately lead to a path forward.

If You Pass Evaluation

  • ADTC issues your Form 5B certificate immediately
  • Collect Form 5B along with your evaluation report
  • Apply to your local RTO with Form 5B + supporting documents
  • RTO verifies documents — no driving test required
  • Driving licence issued directly

If You Do Not Pass

Not passing the ADTC evaluation does not prevent you from getting a licence — it means Form 5B is not issued at this time. You have options:

  • A.Re-enrol in the ADTC course. Get more practice, then attempt the evaluation again.
  • B.Apply directly to the RTO and appear for the standard RTO driving test — the traditional route remains available.

ADTC cannot prevent you from obtaining a licence — only the RTO has that authority.

Documents

Documents Required for Enrolment

Ensure you have the following documents ready before arriving at ADTC Firozabad for registration.

Mandatory for All Categories
  • Valid identity proof — Aadhaar Card / Voter ID / Passport
  • Address proof — Aadhaar / Utility bill / Ration card
  • Recent passport-size photographs (4–6 copies)
  • Medical fitness certificate (Form 1A — from registered medical practitioner)
  • Date of birth proof (school certificate / birth certificate)
For LMV / Commercial Vehicles
  • Valid Learner's Licence for the applicable category
  • For transport/commercial — additional fitness certificates may apply
