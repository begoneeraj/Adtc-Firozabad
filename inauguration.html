ADTC Firozabad Inauguration

A milestone in road safety, driver education, and community service — the official inauguration of ADTC Firozabad, an initiative of Kisaan Eicher Tractors, Shikohabad, in affiliation with the Government of Uttar Pradesh.

Kisaan Eicher Tractors, Shikohabad
Official Announcement

A Proud Milestone for Our Community

It is a matter of immense pride to announce the successful inauguration of the ADTC Centre — a subsidiary of Kisaan Eicher Tractors, Shikohabad, and duly affiliated with the Government of Uttar Pradesh under the Central Motor Vehicles Rules 1989.

The centre is now fully operational, dedicated to providing high-quality, standardized driving training and objective, automated evaluation — upholding excellence and setting a new benchmark for road safety in Firozabad.

We are proud to share snapshots from the inauguration ceremony — a defining moment that signifies our steadfast commitment to progress, innovation, community safety, and service to the citizens of Uttar Pradesh.
